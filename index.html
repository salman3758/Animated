How It Works


ELECTRICITY


Smart Energy Meter delivers your home's energy consumption analytics directly to your smartphone. Insights help you reduce electricity bill significantly. Understanding how energy is consumed is the first step towards managing it. Smart Meter is a robust hardware which takes samples of current and voltage from all phases every single second, makes necessary calculations and transmits the data to our servers.


A portion of the data is then pushed to the mobile application over Internet. Data is also stored on the servers to extract useful insights. So you can always know what's happening in your home even when you are away.
